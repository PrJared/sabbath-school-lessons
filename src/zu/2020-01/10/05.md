---
title:  Umsebenzi kaMesiya
date:  04/03/2020
---

Umthandazo kaDaniyeli wokushweleza uqonde kokubili okukhathazayo: izono zabantu kanye nencithakalo yeJerusalema. Ngakhoke impendulo kaNkulunkulu isabela kulezicelo ezimbili. Ngenxa yomsebenzi kaMesiya, abantu bazosindiswa, nendlu engcwele igcotshwe. Lezicelo ezimbili kodwa ziphendulwa ngezindlela ezidlula lapho amehlo kaDaniyeli engafinyelela khona emlandweni: umsebenzi kaMesiya uyokuba lusizo ebantwini bomhlaba bonke.

`Funda kuDaniyeli 9:21-27. Msebenzi muni owawuzokwenziwa ngamasonto angu 70? Kungani kunguJesu yedwa ongakwenza lokho?`

1. “Ukuqeda iziphambeko.” Igama lesiHeberu elisho iziphambeko (pesha’) lisho isenzo sangamabomu somuntu omncane edelela omkhulu (isibonelo, Izaga 28:24). Leligama liphinde livele eBhayibhelini maqondana nabantu bedelela uNkulunkulu ngokusobala (Hezekeli 2:3). Ngegazi likaJesu, kodwa, ukuhlubuka kuNkulunkulu kuyacinywa, abantu baphiwe ukulunga okuvela eKhalvari.
2. “Nokukhawula izono.” Isenzo simumethe umqondo othi “ukunamathisela” kanti lapha lisho ukuthi isono sixolelwe. Selokho kwangena isono, abantu abakwazanga ukuphila ngokweqophelo elimiswe nguNkulunkulu, kodwa uMesiya uzokulungisa ukwesilela kwethu.
3. “Nokwenza ukubuyisana ngobubi.” UPawulu uyibeka kanje: “Ngokuba kwaba kuhle kuye ukuba ukugcwala konke kuhlale kuyo, nokuba ngayo abuyisane nakho konke esenzile ukuthula ngegazi lesiphambano sayo” (Kolose 1:19, 20). Nalapha futhi, uJesu kuphela ongenza lokhu.
4. “Ukuletha ukulunga kwaphakade.” UKrestu wathatha indawo yethu esiphambanweni, ngaleso senzo wasinika isimo esibusisekile “sokuba abalungileyo” phambi kukaNkulunkulu. Kungokukholwa kuphela lapho singathola khona lokhu kulunga okuvela kuNkulunkulu.
5. “Ukuvala umbono nesiprofetho.” Mhla uKrestu wayezinikela, ezenza umhlatshelo, iziprofetho zeTestamente eliDala ezazikhomba emsebenzini waKhe wokubuyisana zavalwa ngoba zase zigcwalisekile.
6. “Nokugcoba eNgcwele-ngcwele.” Leyo eNgcwele-ngcwele okukhulunywa ngayo lapha akusiye umuntu, kodwa indawo. Ngakhoke lamazwi abhekise ekuqalisweni komsebenzi kaKrestu wokushweleza endlini engcwele yasezulwini (Heb. 8:1).
