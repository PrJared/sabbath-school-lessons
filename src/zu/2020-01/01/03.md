---
title:  Ukuhlelwa Kwencwadi kaDaniyeli
date:  30/12/2019
---

Ukuhlelwa kwesigaba sesi-Arameyiki kuDaniyeli, izahluko 2-7 (izinxenye ezithile zikaDaniyeli zazibhalwe ngesiHeberu, kanti ezinye zazibhalwe ngesi-arameyiki), kutshengisa lokhu kuhlelwa okulandelayo, okuqinisa isigijimi esiyingqikithi yaleso sigaba kanye nencwadi yonkana:

- A. Umbono kaNebukadinesari wemibuso emine (Daniyeli 2)
    - B. UNkululunkulu ukhulula abangane bakaDaniyeli esithandweni somlilo (Daniyeli 3)
        - C. Isahlulelo kuNebukadinesari (Daniyeli 4)
        - C’. Isahlulelo kuBelishasari (Daniyeli 5)
    - B’. UNkulunkulu ukhulula uDaniyeli emphandwini wezingonyama (Daniyeli 6)
- A’. Umbono kaDaniyeli wemibuso emine (Daniyeli 7)

Ukuhlelwa kokubhala ngalendlela kusiza ukugqamisa iphuzu okuyilona elikhulu ngokulibeka enhliziyweni yohlelo, esikubona lapha ngo C no C’ (Daniyeli 4 no 5): UNkulunkulu ususa umbuso kuNebukadinesari (okwesikhashana) nakuBelishasari (unomphelo). Ngakho-ke okugcizelelwa yizahluko 2-7 ubukhosi bukaNkulunkulu phezu kwamakhosi omhlaba njengoba ewabeka aphinde awasuse. Enye yezindlela ezisebenza kahle kakhulu ukudlulisa umlayezo nokucacisa iphuzu, ukuphindaphinda. Isibonelo: uNkulunkulu unika uFaro amaphupho amabili mayelana nokuzokwenzeka eGibhithe masinyane Genesise41:1-7). Ephusheni lokuqala, izinkomo eziyisikhombisa ezikhuluphele zigwinywa eziyisikhombisa ezizacile. Ephusheni lesibili, izikhwebu eziyisikhobisa ezinhle zingwinywa eziyisikhombisa ezihamukile. Womabili lamaphupho asho into eyodwa: iminyaka eyisikhombisa yenala ezolandelwa yiminyaka eyisikhombisa yendlala. Encwadini kaDaniyeli, nalapho uNkulunkulu usebenzisa ukuphindaphinda. Kukhona iziwombe ezine zesiprofetho, okungukuphinwa kohlelo jikelele. Ekugcineni, loluhlelo lusitshengisa ubukhulu bobukhosi bukaNkulunkulu. Nakuba isiprofetho ngasinye sikhuluma ngento eyehlukile, sezihlangene, zikhuluma ngomlando ofanayo, osukela esikhathini somprofethi kuye ekupheleni, njengoba lomfanekiso ongenzansi utshengisa:
