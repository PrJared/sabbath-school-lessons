---
title:  Umbuso Olandelayo
date:  18/03/2020
---

`Funda kuDaniyeli 11:29-39. Yimuphi lombuso ovela ngemuva kweRoma engakholwa na?`

UDaniyeli 11:29-39 ukhuluma ngohlelo olusha lokubusa. Nakuba loluhlelo luqhubeka njalo neRoma engakholwa, futhi lunezinye zezimpawu zombuso ongaphambi kwawo, ubonakala wehlukile kweminye imikhakha. IBhayibheli lithi ngeke ufane neyayingaphambi kwawo, futhi neyolandela (Dan. 11:29). Uma siqhubeka subuka, sithola ukuthi usebenza njengombuso wezenkolo. Uqondise kakhulu ukuhlasela kwawo ebantwini bakaNkulunkulu. Ake sibuke ezinye izenzo zalenkosi.

Okokuqala, “iyothukuthelela isivumelwano esingcwele” (Dan. 11:30). Lokho kuqondise esivumelwaneni sikaNkulunkulu sosindiso, lenkosi esiphikisayo. Okwesibili, lenkosi izokuba nezimpi “eziyongcolisa indlu engcwele” zisuse “umnikelo oshiswa imihla yonke” (Dan. 11:31). Siphawulile kuDaniyeli 8 ukuthi uphondo oluncane luphonsa phansi isisekelo “sendlu engcwele” kaNkulunkulu lususe “nemihlatshelo yemihla yonke” (Dan. 8:11). Lokhu asikuthathe njengokuhlaselwa kwenkonzo eyenziwa nguKrestu endlini engcwele yasezulwini.

Okwesithathu, lenkosi ibeka “isinengiso esichithayo” ethempelini likaNkulunkulu. Uma kubekwa ngenye indlela ethi “isiphambeko esichithayo” kukhomba izenzo zohlubuko lophondo oluncane (Dan. 8:13). Okwesine, lenkosi uhlupha abantu bakaNkulunkulu: “abanye kwabahlakaniphileyo bayakuwa ukuba bacwengisiswe, bahlanzwe, benziwe mhlophe kuze kube sesikhathini sokuphela” (Dan. 11:35). Lokhu kusikhumbuza uphondo oluncane, olwalahlela phansi okunye kwebandla, okwezinkanyezi, lwakunyathela (Dan. 8:10; qhathanisa no Dan. 7:25). Okwesihlanu, lenkosi “izoziphakamisa, izikhulise phezu kwabo bonke onkulunkulu, ikhulume okumangalisayo ngoNkulunkulu wawonkulunkulu” (Dan. 11:36). Akumangalisi ukuthi uphondo oluncane lukhuluma “amazwi ngoPhezukonke” (Dan. 7:8), luhlambalaze noNkulunkulu (Dan. 7:25).

`Singakhuluma nangokunye okufanayo, kodwa ngenxa yalokho esikufunde kuDaniyeli 7 no 8, ubani lenkosi, futhi kubaluleke ngani kithina, ukuthi nakuba kunezingcindezi emphakathini, singangabazi ukuyisho ukuthi ingubani?`
