Ukukhanya kwangaphakathi kukaMoya oNgcwele, bembula emphefumulweni ukungabi nathemba kwawo, nesimo sosizi ngaphandle kwesihe kanye nentethelelo yoMthwali-zono—uKristu oyikho konke—nguyena kuphela owenza abantu bakwazi ukubona isihe Sakhe esingapheliyo, uthando Lwakhe olungenasilinganiso, ubumnene, kanye nenkazimulo.”—Ellen G. White, The Upward Look, p. 155. “Izingxenye zeZibhalo, noma izahluko eziphelele, zingafakwa ekhanda, ukuze ziphindwe ngenkathi uSathane eza nezilingo zakhe.... **Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`1. Iqiniso lokukwazi ukuzikhethela lingena kuphi ezinqumeni zethu zonke mayelana nokholo nakulalela na? Nakuba izigaba eziningi zempilo yethu singakwazi ukuzilawula, mayelana nezinto ezibucayi, izinto eziphathelene Nokuphila okuphakade, sinelungelo lokuzikhethela. Wenzani ngalelolungelo lokuzikhethela olinikezwe nguNkulunkulu na? Kukhetha kuni kwezikamoya okwenzayo na?`

`2. Cabanga ngendima iSabatha elingayidlala nekumele liyidlale mayelana nokusinikeza isikhathi esithule noNkulunkulu. Ukugcina iSabatha kukuvikela kanjani ekuxakekeni okukwenza ungakwazi ukuchitha isikhathi osidingayo noNkulunkulu na? Ungafunda kanjani ukwenza iSabatha libe yisibusiso kakhulu kwezikamoya okumele libe yiso na?`

`3. Abenjani amava akho ekuchitheni isikhathi wedwa noNkulunkulu ekukhulekeni nasekufundeni na? Lomkhuba wezikamoya uluthinta kanjani ukholo lwakho na? Kumele uluthinte kanjani ukholo lwakho na? Eklasini, uma kukuhle kuwe, khuluma ngezikhathi zakho zokufunda nokukhuleka nangalokho okuzuzile kuzo. Abanye bangazuzani kulokho okufundile na?`

`4. Yiziphi iZibhalo eziyintandokazi kuwe ozifake ekhanda na? Yini ngazo okuthanada kangaka na? Ukuzazi kwakho ngekhanda kube yisibusiso kanjani kuwe na?`
