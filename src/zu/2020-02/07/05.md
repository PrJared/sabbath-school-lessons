---
title:  Imisho NeziNgqikithi
date:  13/05/2020
---

Amagama Ezibhalweni njalo aqhamuka ngokwengqikithi. Awazimeli wodwa. Igama linengqikithi yalo ngokwengqikithi yomusho, futhi yilesigaba okumele siqondwe kuqala. Bese kuba khona ingqikithi ebanzi yesigaba esibanzi somusho. Lokhu kungaba yisigaba sombhalo, Isahluko, noma uhla lwezahluko. Kusemqoka ukuqonda futhi ingqikithi okungase kube yiyo yamagama nemisho ukuze singafinyeleli eziphethweni eziphambukileyo.

`Qhathanisa uGenesisi 1:27 no Genesisi 2:7. Bese ufunda uGenesisi 2:15-23. Kulezizahluko ezahlukene singayiqonda kanjani ingqikithi yencazelo kaAdamu, igama lesiHeberu lo “muntu” na?`

Sesibonile ukuthi ukuphindaphindwa kwegama baba’ kuGenesisi 1:27 kukhomba isigcizelelo ekudalweni kumuntu. Manje sibona ukuthi umuntu uchazwa ngokwengqikithi yalendimana njengo-“wesilisa nowesifazane” lokhu Kuchaza ukuthi itemu lesiHeberu u¬adam kumele liqondwe kulesisahluko njengelibhekise kwisintu.  Kepha, kuGenesisi 2:7 lelitemu adam lisetshenziswe ekubhekiseni ekwakhiweni kukaAdamu ngothuli “lomhlaba” (kwisiHeberu Adamah—qaphela ukudlala ngamagama). Lapha nguAdamu wesilisa kuphela okubhekiswe kuye, ngokuba uEva akadaliwe kuze kube kamuva futhi ngendlela ehluke kakhulu. Ngalokho, kwisahluko ngasinye, nakuyo ingqikithi yezahluko ezimbili, sibona ukwehlukanisa phakathi kwencazelo ka-adam njenge-“sintu” (Gen. 1:27) kanye nendoda uAdamu (Gen. 2:7). Ukuthi uAdam ngumuntu kuqiniswa ezizukulwaneni (Gen. 5:1–5, 1 Chron. 1:1, Luke 3:38) kanye nasekubhekiseni kuJesu, oba “nguAdam wesibili” (Rom. 5:12–14). Njengalokhu igama uAdam liqhamuka ngokombhalo othile, ngalokho ingqikithi yokudalwa kukaAdam noEva itholakala embikweni Obanzi woKudala Njengalokhu kubonakala kuGenesisi 1-2. Yilokho okuchazwa ngesigaba esibanzi. Lesisigaba sitshela umchasisi ngezindikimba ezengeziwe, imiqondo, kanye nokuthuthuka. UGenesisi 2:4-25 ubizwe ngezinye izikhathi njengombiko wesibili wokudala, kodwa Empeleni kunokugcizelela okwehlukile (buka iviki elilandelayo). Kuyo yomibili lemibiko, nokho, siboniswa indabuko eqinisekile yesintu.

`Njengoba sibona, Indoda nomfazi—isintu—bayizidalwa zikaNkulunkulu ngokuqondile. Lokhu kusitshelani ngobuwula “benhlakanipho yalomhlaba” (1 Kor. 1:20) eyibo ngempela ngokusifundisa ukuthi sabakhona ngokungalindelekile na?`
