Funda u-Ellen G. White, “What to Do With Doubt,” pp. 105–113, in Steps to Christ, and from the document “Methods of Bible Study” Section 1: “Bible Study: Presuppositions, Principles, and Methods”, Section 2: “Presuppositions Arising from the Claims of Scripture” and Section 3: “Principles for Approaching the Interpretation of Scripture.” (Methods of Bible Study ingatholaka ku: www.adventistbiblicalresearch.org/materials/bible-interpretationhermeneutics/methods-bible-study). “Ekufundeni kwakho izwi, shiya emnyango wokuhlola Ukucabangela kwakho kanye nemicabango yokho yofuzo. Ngeke ufinyelele eqinisweni uma ufunda Izibhalo ukuvikela imicabango yakho. Shiya lezi emnyango, futhi ngenhliziyo ezisolayo ngena ukuyozwa lokho iNkosi efuna ukukusho kuwe. Njengomcingi othobekile weqiniso Ehlezi ezinyaweni zikaKristu, futhi efunda Kuye, izwi limnikeza ukuqonda. Kulabo abahlakaniphe kakhulu ekuqhosheni kwabo ukuba bafunde iBhayibheli, uKristu uthi, Kumele ubemnene futhi uthobeke enhliziyweni uma ufisa ukuhlakaniphela ekusindisweni. “Musa ukufunda izwi ngokokukhanya kwemicabango yakho yakuqala; kodwa, ngengqondo engenako Ukucabangela, lihlole ngokuqikelela nangokukhuleka. Uma, nengoba ufunda, ukukholwa kuza, futhi ubona ukuthi ubunemicabango engahambisani nezwi, ungazami ukwenza izwi lihambisane nalemicabango. Yenza imicabango yakho ihambisane nezwi. Ungavumeli lokho okukholiwe futhi kwaba ngumkhuba ngaphambilini kulawule ukuqonda kwakho. Vula amehlo engqondo yakho ukubona izinto ezimangalisayo emthethweni. Thola ukuthi yini elotshiweyo, bese ugxilisa izinyawo zakho Edwaleni laphakade.”—Ellen G. White, Messages to Young People, p. 260.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`1. Ukubuka kwethu kwezwe, imfundo yethu, kanye nesiko-mpilo lethu kunethonya elinjani ekuchasiseni kwethu KweZibhalo na? Kungani kubaluke kakhulu ukuba siqwashe ngamathonya angaphandle esiza nawo ekuchasiseni kwethu kweBhayibheli na?`

`2. Siyavumelana sonke ngokuthi siyizoni futhi sithinteke kabi yisono. Yiziphi izindlela isono esithinta ngayo indlela esifunda ngayo iBhayibheli na? Okungukuthi, senzani isono kithina okungenza ukuba singachasisi ngokufanele Izwi likaNkulunkulu na? Ngokwesibonelo, ukufisa kwethu ukwenza into engafunwa eBhayibhelini kungasenza kanjani ukuba sifunde iBhayibheli ngendlela ephambukile na? Yiziphi ezinye izindlela isono esisefa ngazo ukuchasisa kwethu iBhayibheli na?`

`3. Ukuqonda okungcono kwezikhathi zeBhayibheli kanye nesiko-mpilo kungasisiza kanjani ukuqonda ezinye izahluko zeZibhalo na? Nikeza izibonelo.`
