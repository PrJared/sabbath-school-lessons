---
title:  Bhekana Nobunzima Ngokuzithoba
date:  16/06/2020
---

`Funda uJakobe 4:6–10; 2 IziKronike 7:14; no Zefaniya 3:12. Kungani ukuthobeka kubalulekile lapho sizama ukubhekana nezahluko ezilukhuni Ezibhalweni na?`

Abantu abaningi bafinyelele ekuboneni okumangalisayo kanye nokuthobayo bokuthi bancike phezu kokuthile kanye nakomunye ongaphandle kwabo. Babona ukuthi abasona isilinganiso sakho konke. Lababantu bathathela phezulu iqiniso ngaphezu kwesidingo sikamina sokuba ngomuhle, futhi bayabona ukuthi iqiniso akulona elokwenza kwabo kodwa, kunalokho, ngelalokho abanqwamana nako. Mhlawumbe okuyilona qiniso elikhulu kunawo onke ngelokuthi lababanu bayaqonda ukuthi Empeleni kuncane kakhulu abakwaziyo ngeqiniso. Bayazi, njengalokhu kwaloba uPawule, ukuthi “babona kalufifi ngesibuko, kumnyamana” (1 Cor. 13:12).

Inzuzo yalokhu kuzithoba ekucabangeni inkulu: umkhuba wokuhlola ngokuzithoba uyisisekelo sakho konke ukukhula olwazini, ngokuba kuphehla inkululeko okuthi ngemvelo idale umoya ofundisekayo. Lokhu akuchazi ukuthi abantu abathobekile bavama ukuba sephutheni, noma ukuthi bayolokhu beguqula izingqondo futhi bengeke baqina ekuvumeni. Kusho kuphela ukuthi bayazinkela eqinisweni leBhayibheli. Bayakwazi ukusilela kwabo olwazini futhi, ngalokho, bayakwazi ukwandisa ulwazi kanye nokuqonda kwabo iZwi likaNkulunkulu ngendlela umuntu ohlakaniphile, eqhosha, eziqhenya, angakwazi ngayo. “Bonke abeza Ezwini likaNkulunkulu ukwelulekwa, ngezingqondo ezithobekile, nezifuna ukwazi, bezimisele ukwazi iminingwane yosindiso, bayoqonda lokho okushiwo yiZibhalo. Kepha labo abeza nokuhlola Izwi ngomoya ongavumi, bayothola lokho umoya ongabanikanga kona. Inkosi ayisayikukhuluma nomqondo ongakhathaliyo. Ayichithi iseluleko sayo kulowo ongahloniphi futhi engcole ngokuvuma kwakhe. Kodwa umlingi ufundisa zonke izingqondo ezizinikele ezincomeni zakhe futhi zizimisele ukwenza ize umthetho ongcwele kaNkulunkulu.

“Sidinga izinhliziyo ezithobekileyo, ngokukholeka kanye nokuhlola okuzothile Izwi lokuphila; ngokuba ileyongqondo kuphela ethobekile nephendukile engabona ukukhanya.”—The Advent Review and Sabbath Herald, August 22, 1907.

`Ulinganisa kanjani ngokufanele phakathi kokuzithoba kanye nokuqiniseka na? Isibonelo, ungawuphendula kanjani umgixelo othi, nina ma-Seventh-day Adventist ningaqinisekea kanjani ukuthi niqinisile mayelana neSabatha nokuthi bonke abanye baphambukile na?`
