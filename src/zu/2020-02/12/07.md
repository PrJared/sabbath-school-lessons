Funda u-Ellen G. White, “What to Do With Doubt?” pp. 105–113 in Steps to Christ. Read section 8 in the Document “Methods of Bible Study,” engatholakala ku: http:// www.adventistbiblicalresearch.org/materials/bible-interpretation-hermeneutics/methods bible-study. EBhayibhelini ziningi izimfihlakalo isintu esizithola zilukhuni ukuziqonda futhi ezidephe kakhulu kithina ukuba sizichaze ngokuphelele. Yingako sidinga umqondo othobekile, futhi kumele sizimisele ukufunda Izibhalo sikhuleka. Ukuthembeka Ezibhalweni kwenza umbhalo WeBhayibheli—nakuba incazelo yayo iphikisana nathi— usho lokho ewushoyo. Ukuthembeka Ezibhalweni kuyohlonipha lokho okushiwo yimbhalo kunokuwuguqula (yebo, abanye bawuguqula ngokwabo umbhalo) noma bagweme incazelo yawo yeqiniso. “Ngenkathi izwi likaNkulunkulu livulwa ngokungahloniphi nangokungakhuleki; lapho imicabango kanye nezinkanuko zingagxilile kuNkulunkulu, noma zingavumelani nentando Yakhe, inqgqondo igcwala ukungabaza; futhi kukho konke ukufundwa kweBhayibheli, ukungabaza kuqine. Izitha silawula imicabango, futhi silethe izichasiso okungezona. Njalo uma abantu ngokukhuluma nangokwenza bengafuni ukuhambisana noNkulunkulu, ngalokho, nakuba befunde kangakanani, besengcuoheni yokuphambuka ekuqondeni kwabo Izibhalo, futhi akuphephile ukwethemba izincazelo zabo. Labo ababheka Ezibhalweni ukuthola amaphutha, abanako ukubona kwezikamoya. Ngokubuka okuphendukezelwe bazobona izizathu eziningi zokungabaza ngokungakholwa ezintweni ezilula nezisobala.”—Ellen G. White, Steps to Christ, pp. 110, 111.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`1. Kungani imimoya yethu yokubuka iBhayibheli esikhulume ngayo kuleliviki iyisisekela esikhulu ekuqondeni kwethu Izibhalo ngokufanele na? Yimiphi eminye imimoya ngakweBhayibheli okholwa ukuthi isemqoka ekusizeni wena ukuba uliqonde kangcono na?`

`2. Kungani kungamele simangale lapho sithola izinto eBhayibheli okulukhuni ukuzichaza nokuziqonda na? Nembala, zingaki izinto endalweni okuthi ngezinye izikhathi kube lukhuni ukuziqonda na? kuze kube namuhla, ngokwesibonelo,` amanzi (amanzi!) angcwele izimfihlo.

`3. Njengama-Adventist, singawuphendula kanjani umbuzo kaLuka 23:34, lapho (ngokwezihumusho eziningi) uJesu utshela isela ukuthi liyoba noJesu ezulwini ngalolosuku na? Yiziphi izindlela eziqotho zokuphendula na? Kungenzeka kanjani, ngokwesibonelo, ukuba imibhalo enjengoJohane 20:17, Mshumayeli 9:5, no 1 Korinte 15:16–20 isisize ekuqondeni udaba lwalombuzo na?`
