---
title:  Izwi LikaNkulunkulu Elilotshiweyo
date:  07/04/2020
---

`“UJehova wathi kuMose: ‘Zilobele lawa mazwi, ngokuba ngokwalawa mazwi ngenzile isivumelwano nawe nabakwa-Israyeli’” (Exod. 34:27, NKJV). Kungani uNkulunkulu afuna ukuba uMose alobe phansi lamazwi kunokuba uMose awasho ngomlomo kuphela kubantu na? Iyiphi inzuzo esobala yeZwi Elilotshiweyo na?`

UNkulunkulu okhulumayo futhi nowadala ulimi lwesintu wenza abantu abakhethekile ukuba badlulise amaqiniso ambuliwe obunkulunkulu kanye nemicabango ephefumulelwe ngobunkulunkulu ngendlela ethembekile kanye neqotho. Yingakho, kungamangalisi ukuthola ukuthi uNkulunkulu wayaleza abalobi beZibhalo kwasekuqaleni ukuba babhale phansi imiyalezo Yakhe kanye nesambulo Sakhe.

**Izindimana ezilandelayo zifundisani ngeSambulo esilotshiweyo na?**

`Eksod. 17:14; 24:4`

`Josh. 24:26`

`Jer. 30:2`

`Samb.1:11, 19; 21:5; 22:18, 19`

Kungani uNkulunkulu ayaleza ukuba Isambulo Sakhe kanye nemibiko ephefumulelwe ilotshwe phansi na? Impendulo esobala ngukuthi singazikhohlwa kalula. Amazwi alotshiweyo eBhayibheli angukudlulisela okungaphezi okusikhomba kuNkulunkulu kanye nentando Yakhe. Incwadi elotshiweyo ingalondeka kangcono futhi ibeqotho kakhulu kunemibiko yomlomo, okumele ishiwo ngokuphindelela. Izwi Elilotshiweyo, elingakopishwa njalo, njalo, lingenziwa futhi lifinyeleleke kubantu abaningi kunokuba belikhulunywa nje kuphela. Ekugcineni, singakhuluma kubantu abalinganisiwe ngesikhathi esisodwa endaweni eyodwa, kodwa lokho okuthe kwalotshwa kungafundwa ngabantu abangenakubalwa ezindaweni eziningi nakumazwekazi, kuze kube yisibusiso kwizizukulwane eziningi kamuva. Empeleni, uma bengakwazi ukuzifundela ngokwabo, abanye bangabafundela okubhaliwe kakhulu.
