Iphuzu elibucayi okumele silibone lapha ngukuthi umbono wokwahlulela kuDaniyeli 7, okwenzeka ngemuva kweminyaka engu-1,260 yokushushiswa (Dan. 7:25), uyafana nokuhlanzwa kwendawo engcwele kuDaniyeli 8:14. Futhi lokhu kwahlulela esikubona ezulwini yikona okuholela, ekugcineni, ekusungulweni kombuso kaNkulunkulu waphakade ekupheleni komlando odabukisayo walomhlaba owile. Yingakho, sinobufakazi obunamandla bezebhayibheli bokubaluleka okukhulu Izibhalo ezikubeka kuDaniyeli 8:14 kanye nesigameko osiphawulayo.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`1. Buyela emuva futhi ubuyekeze uDaniyeli 2. Buka ukuba sobala kwendlela yomlando okwembulwe lapha: ukulandelana okungaphazamisekile kwemibuso yomhlaba, kusukela emandulo kuze kugcine ngokusekwa kombuso kaNkulunkulu waphakade. UNkulunkulu usinikeza ukhiye wokuchasisa leziziprofetho. Kuthini, nokho, mayelana nesimo sobuKristu ukuthi ambalwa kakhulu AmaKristu namuhla asebenzisa lendlela yomlando na? Kungani leliqiniso lisiza ekusunguleni ukufaneleka okukhulu kombiko wama-Adventist emhlabeni ngalesisikhathi na?`

`2. Usiqonda kahle kangakanani isiprofetho sezinsuku ezingu-2,300 sikaDaniyeli 8:14 na? Uma ungasiqondi, kungani ungathathi isikhathi usifunde futhi wabelane ngaso neklasi na? Ungase umangaliswe ngukuzinza okuqinile kwesichasiso sethu salesosiprofetho.`

`3. Funda uDaniyeli 7:18, 21, 22, 25, 27. Qaphela ukugxila kulokho okwenzeka kwabangcwele. Umbuso wophondo oluncane wenzani kubo na? Ngokumelene, uJehovah ubenzelani na? Yiziphi izindaba ezilungileyo kwabangcwele mayelana nokwahlulela na? Ekugcineni lokhu kwahlulela kubanikezani na?`
