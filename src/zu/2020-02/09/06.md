**Funda izibhalo ezilandelayo bese ubhala indlela Umbhali ngamunye abhekise ngayo kuGenesisi 1–11:**

`Mat. 19:4, 5`

`Marko 10:6–9`

`Luka 11:50, 51`

`Johane 1:1–3`

`Zenzo 14:15`

`Rom. 1:20`

`2 Kor. 4:6`

`Efes. 3:9`

`1 Tim. 2:12–15`

`Jakobe 3:9`

`1 Pet. 3:20`

`Juda 11, 14`

`Samb. 2:7; 3:14; 22:2, 3`

UJesu kanye nabo bonke ababhali beTestamente Elisha babhekisa kuGenesisi 1-11 njengomlando othembekileyo. UJesu ubhekisa Emibhalweni kaMose kanye nasekudalweni kowesilisa ngowesifazane (Mat. 19:4). Ngokuphindaphinda uPawule usebenzisa umbiko wokudala ukuqinisa amaphuzu akhe emfundiso awenzayo ezincwadini zakhe. Wamemezela kumadoda afundile ase-Athens, “UNkulunkulu owenza umhlaba nakho konke okukuwo, yena oyiNkosi yezulu nomhlaba, akahlali emathempelini enziwe ngezandla” (Zenzo 17:24, NASB). Ngalezizindlela, ababhali beTestamente Elisha bakhela phezu kwesimo sikaGenesisi esiyisekelo ukubonisa umfundi wanamuhla ukubaluleka kwalesisigameko sangempela. Funda, ngokwesibonelo, abaseRoma 5. Izikhathi ezevile kweziyisithupha, uPawule wenza ukuxhumanisa okuqondile kusukela kuAdamu kuye kuJesu (buka abaseRoma 5:12, 14-19). Okungukuthi, uvuma ukubakhona ngokoqobo kukaAdamu Womlando, isikhundla esibasengcupheni kakhulu uma imiqondo yokusombuluka kwendalo ithatha indawo yokoqobo yokufunda imibhalo.
