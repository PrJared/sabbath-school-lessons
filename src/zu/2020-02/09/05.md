---
title:  Ukudala kanye Nesikhathi
date:  27/05/2020
---

`Funda uGenesisi 5 no 11. IBhayibheli liwuthungatha kanjani umlando wesintu kusukela kuAdamu kuye kuNowa, kanye nasekusukeni kuNowa kuye kuAbrahama na?`

Kukhona okuthile okwenza izizukulwane zehluke eBhayibhelini: ziqukethe into yesikhathi, okwenza abanye abafundi ukuba bazibize “ngezizukulwane zesikhathi.” Ziqukethe izakhi ezixubene zolwazi oluphusile oluhlangene nezikhala zesikhathi, ukuze “ngenkathi Umuntu Wokuqala esephile iminyaka engu-x, waba nguyise  kaMuntu Wesibili. Futhi emuva kokuthi Umuntu Wokuqala ezele Umuntu Wesibili waphila iminyaka engu-y, futhi waba namanye amadodana namadodakazi.” UGenesisi 5 wengeza isisho esiyi-formula, “Futhi zonke izinsuku zoMuntu Wokuqala zaba ngu-z weminyaka.” Lokhu kukhiyelana bekungavimbela ukucisha izizukulwana ezithile noma ekwengezeni ezithile. UGenesisi 5 no 11 baqukethe uhla oluqhubekayo lwezizukulwane, Njengalokhu kuvumelana no-1 IziKronike 1:18-27, lapho kungeko zizukulwane ezengeziwe noma ezingekho. Ngaleyondlela iBhayibheli liyazichasisa ngokwalo

Cishe iminyaka engu-2,000, abahlaziyi bamaJuda kanye nabamaKrsitu bachasise lemibhalo ngokuthi imele umlando futhi iyindlela yokuthola kahle ukuthi Uzamcolo wabakhona nini kanye nobudala bomhlaba, okungenani kusukela ezinsukwini eziyisikhombisa zoKudala Njengalokhu kusho uGenesisi 1-2. Eminyakeni engamashumi edlule, kubekhona imizamo yokuchasisa kabusha uGenesisi 5 no 11 ukuvumela izikhathi ezinde, njengalokho olunye ulwazi lomlando kanye nolwemivumbukulo ichasiswa (ngabantu abanamaphutha) income. Lokhu kuvusa Imibuzo eqinile mayelana nokwethmebeka kombiko WeBhayibheli. Kodwa uma siqonde umqondo kaNkulunkulu wesikhathi kanye nokuhamba kwaso emlandweni, kumele sivume ukuthi lezizahluko ezimbili “zingumlando kanye nemfundiso, exhmanisa uAdamu kanye nesintu sonkana kanye noNkulunkulu nomuntu embusweni ofinyelela esikhaleni nasesikhathini. UGenesisi 5 no 11:10-26 bahlinzeka ngesikhathi kanye nekentanga lesintu elixhumanisa abantu bakaNkulunkulu kanye nomuntu uNkulunkulu amdala njengovuthondaba lokudala ngezinsuku eziyisithupha kwalomhlaba.”—Gerhard F. Hasel, “The Meaning of the Chronogenealogies of Genesis 5 and 11,” Origins 7/2 [1980], p. 69.

`Nakuba lezizahluko kwiTestamente Elidala zikhona ngezizathu ezinhle nezibalulekile, uPawule uthini ku-1 Timothewu 1:4 nakuTitu 3:9 okumele sikuqaphele lapho sikhuluma ngezindimana ezinjena na?`
