### Funda Kuleliviki
Mat. 4:1–11; Mat. 22:37–40; Luka 24:13–35, 44, 45; Luka 4:25–27; Zenzo 4:24–26.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Kepha waphendula wathi: ‘Kulotshiwe ukuthi: ‘Akusinkwa sodwa umuntu ayakuphila ngaso kodwa ngamazwi onke aphuma emlonyeni kaNkulunkulu” ’ ” (Matthew 4:4, NKJV).

Ngeshwa, kulesisikhathi sanamuhla, iBhayibheli selihumushwe kakhulu ngesibuko sefilosofi engabaza ukuphefumulelwa kwalo kanye negunya lalo. Empeleni, iBhayibheli libukwa njengemiqondo nje yabantu ababephila ngesikhathi sasendulo abangenako ukuqonda izwe esiphila kulo namuhla. Kusenjalo, umkhakha walokho okungekona okwasemhlabeni kudicilelwe phansi noma kwasuswa ngokuphelele esithombeni, kwalenza iBhayibheli laba yincwadi okuthi, kunokuba ibe yindlela uNkulunkulu abuka ngayo umuntu, yaba yindlela umuntu abuka ngayo uNkulunkulu. Futhi umphumela ngukuthi, kwabaningi, iBhayibheli seliphenduke laba yize esikhathi semiqondo ka-Darwin kanye nokuhlakanipha kwesimanje. Nakuba kunjalo, siwuchitha ngokuphelele lomqondo. Kunalokho, kwiTestamente Elisha, siyabona indlela ephefumulelwe yokubuka Izibhalo ngokufunda indlela uJesu nabapostoli ababeqonda ngayo iTestamente Elidala, okwakuyilona Bhayibheli ababenalo ngalesosikhathi. Babexhumana kanjani nabantu, nezindawo kanye nezigameko ezichaziwe na? Kwakuyiziphi izingathekiso zabo kanye nezindlela zabo zokuhumusha na? Masibalandele bona kanye nokuqonda kwabo, ngokumelene nokungaqondi kwesintu esingaphefumulelwe okungathekisa kwaso kuholela kuphela ekungabazeni Izwi likaNkulunkulu.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, 18 April._
