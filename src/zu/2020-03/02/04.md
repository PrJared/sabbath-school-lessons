---
title:  Impilo Eguquliwe Yenza Umehluko
date:  07/07/2020
---

“Kwathi bebona isibindi sikaPetru noJohane, beqonda ukuthi bangabantu abangafundile, bayimifunda nje, bamangala, babazi ukuthi kade benoJesu” (IZenzo 4:13).

Ibandla lesikhathi seTestamente eliSha landlondlobala ngokukhula. Kwabhabhadiswa izinkulungwane ezintathu ngePentekoste (IZenzo 2:41). Kwenezelwa ezinye izinkulungwane emasontweni alandelayo (IZenzo 4:4).  Kungekudala, iziphathimandla zabona ukuthi kwenzekani.  Labazalwane beTestamente eliSha babekade benoKrestu. Izimpilo zabo zaziguquliwe. Babeguqulwe ngomusa waKhe, abakwazanga ukuthula.

`Funda kuZenzo 4:1-20. Kwenzekani lapha? Kwenzekani uma iziphathimandla zizama ukuthulisa uPetru noJohane? Baphendula bathini?`

Labazalwane babebasha kuKrestu, futhi kwakudingekile ukuthi bayimemezele indaba yabo. UPetru, umdobi owayekhulumela futhi, waguqulwa ngomusa kaNkulunkulu. UJakobe noJohane, amadodana okuduma ababengakwazi ukubamba ulaka lwabo, baguqulwa ngomusa kaNkulunkulu. UThomase ongabazayo waguqulwa ngomusa kaNkulunkulu. Abafundi namalunga ebandla lokuqala bonke babenendaba abangayixoxa. Babengeke bakwazi ukuthula. Qaphela lamazwi anohlonze kaEllen G. White encwadini iSteps to Christ: “Masinyane-nje ngemuva kokuthi umuntu ehlangene noKrestu, kuba khona enhliziyweni yakhe ukufisa abanye ukuthi usethole umngane oyigugu kangakanani onguJesu; iqiniso elisindisayo nelingcwelisayo alikwazi ukuvalelwa enhliziyweni yakhe.”— Ikhasi 78. Qaphela futhi, ukuthi abaholi kwezenkolo bathini endimaneni ka 16.  Basivuma ngokusobala isimangaliso esasenzekile—umuntu owayephulukisiwe wayemi phambi kwabo. Naphezu kwakho konke lokhu, benqaba ukuguqula imiqondo yabo.  Kodwa, phezu kwalokho kunganaki, uPetru noJohane babengazimisele ukuhlehla ebufakazini babo.

`Kunabudlelwane buni phakathi kokwazi uKrestu nokwabelana ngoKrestu? Kungani ukwazi uKrestu kudingeke kangaka kithina ukuze sikwazi ukufakaza ngaYe?`
