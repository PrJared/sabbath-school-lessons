---
title:  Ukwabelana Ngamava Ethu
date:  08/07/2020
---

KuZenzo 26, sithola umphostoli uPawulu eyisiboshwa, emi phambi kwenkosi uAgripha. Lapho, ekhuluma eqondise-ngqo enkosini, uPawulu wenza obakhe ubufakazi, ekhuluma ngempilo yakhe, kungeyona kuphela yalowo owayehlupha abalandeli bakaKrestu; kodwa futhi ngemuva kokuququka kwakhe, ekhuluma ngempilo yakhe njengofakazi kaJesu, nesithembiso sovuko kwabafileyo (IZenzo 26:8). Mhla uPawulu ephenduka endleleni eya eDamaseku, iNkosi yethu yakhuluma kuye yathi: “Ngibonakale kuwe ngalokho ukuba ngikumise ube yisikhonzi nofakazi walokhu engizakuzibonakalisa kuwe ngakho” (IZenzo 26:16). Ukwabelana ngokukholwa kwethu kuhlale kuyinto enamandla. Ukumemezela indaba yalokho uKrestu asenzele kona ngesikhathi esedlule, lokho akwenzayo ezimpilweni zethu namhlanje, nalokho azosenzela kona esikhathini esizayo. Ukufakaza akuphathelene nathi. Njalo-nje kuphathelene naYe. UnguNkulunkulu uthethelela ububi bethu, ophilisa izifo zethu, osiqhelisa ngothando nomusa, asenelise ngezinto ezinhle (IHubo 103:3-5). Ukufakaza ukwabelana ngendaba yethu yomusa omangalisayo. Ubufakazi bokuhlangana kwethu naloNkulunkulu onomusa omangalisayo.

`Funda ku 1 Johane 1:1-3, uqhathanise namaGalatiya 2:20. Yikuphi okufanayo okubonayo? Amava kaJohane afana kanjani nakaPawulu?`

Nakuba uJohane noPawulu babenamava angafani empilweni, bobabili bahlangana noJesu. Amava abo noJesu akusiwo lawo enzeka ngesikhathi esithile esedlule, bese kuyaphela njalo. Ayeqhubeka, zonke izinsuku bethokoza othandweni lwaKhe, behamba ekukhanyeni kweqiniso laKhe.

Singasho yini ukuthi ukuphenduka into yesikhathi esedlule kuphela? Ake ubuke lamazwi ka Ellen G. White mayelana nalabo ababecabanga ukuthi ukuphenduka kwabo esikhathini esedlule kwanele: “Kungathi njengoba kukhona ulwazi abake baluthola ngenkolo ngesinye isikhathi, abasadingi ukuphenduka nsuku zonke; kodwa sonke, zonke izinsuku, sidinga ukuphenduka.”—Manuscript Releases, vol. 4, ikhasi 46.

`Ngokungakhathalekile ukuthi ayenjani amava akho ngesikhathi esingaphambili, noma wawunamandla unyakazisa izintaba, kubaluleke ngani ukuba nobudlelwane neNkosi usuku ngosuku, uzwe ubukhona baYo nokulunga namandla aYo usuku ngosuku na? Woza nempendulo yakho eklasini ngeSabatha.`
