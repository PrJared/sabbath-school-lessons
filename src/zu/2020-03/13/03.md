---
title:  Ubizo Lokuzinikela
date:  21/09/2020
---

Zicabange unguPetru noJohane. Ilanga lisanda kuphuma ngosuku oluhle lwaseGalile, lixosha umoya obandayo wasebusuku. Imicabango yakho igxile entweni eyodwa: ukubamba izinhlanzi, eziningi futhi. Ukudoba bekuhamba kahle kamuva-nje, manje wethemba olunye usuku oluhle lokudoba. Kwase kuthi-ke ngokukhanya kwentathakusa, uyambona eza, uJesu waseNazaretha. Awuzange ucabange ukuthi emizuzwini embalwa yonke impilo oyakho izoguquka. Ngeke uphinde ufane nakuqala.

`Funda kuMathewu 4:18-20. Ucabanga ukuthi kungani uPetru noJohane bavuma ukwenza lokhu kuzinikela okungaka kokulandela uKrestu?  Yini kulendimana etshengisa ukuthi uJesu wayebabizela kokukhulu kunokubamba izinhlanzi?`

Ngokufunda evangelini likaJohane siyathola ukuthi lamadoda ayesenolwazana ngoJesu isikhathi esingaphezu konyaka, kodwa ayengazange azinikele kuYena.  Noma kunjalo, kufanele ukuthi kwakukhona ukuziphatha ngendlela engcwele kuJesu, okuthile ngokubukeka kwaKhe, amazwi, nezenzo, okwatshengisa labadobi baseGalile ukuthi ubabizela obizweni oluphakeme.  Isizathu sokushiya izikebhe zabo, umsebenzi abaziphilisa ngawo, nendawo abayejwayele baze bamlandele ukuthi bezwa ngaphakathi ukuthi ubabizela emsebenzini omkhulu. Labadobi babona ukuthi babizelwe injongo engejwayelekile. Kungenzeka ukuthi uNkulunkulu akakubizi ukuthi ulahle umsebenzi wakho namhlanje, kodwa uyakubiza ukuthi wenze umsebenzi ongajwayelekanga—ukwabalena ngothando lwaKhe nokufakaza ngeqiniso laKhe ukuze kukhazimuliswe igama laKhe.

Cabanga ngobizo lukaMathewu, umqoqi wentela, kuMathewu 9:9.  Yini oyibonayo kulendimana ethathekisa kakhulu?

Abaqoqi bentela kusabusa amaRoma, babaziwa ngenkohlakalo, besebenzisa amandla abo ombuso ukucindezela abantukazana. Babezondwa kakhulu futhi bebukelwa phansi kulo lonke elakwa-Israyeli. Isimemo sikaKrestu esithi: “Ngilandele” sisitshela ukuthi uMathewu wayeseke wezwa ngoJesu, enhliziyweni yakhe waba nokufisa ukumlandela. Kuthe sekufika isimemo, wayesekulungele. Wamangala ngokuthi uKrestu amamukele, ammeme ukuba omunye wabafundi baKhe. Ekujuleni kwezinhliziyo zethu sonke kukhona ukulangazelela okuthile okungaphezulu. Nathi siyafuna ukuphilela into elusizo, enhle, enenhloso ephakeme. Kanjalo-ke uKrestu uyasibiza njengoMathewu ukuba simlandele.

`Cabanga ngalokho abantu abehlukana nakho ukuze balandele uJesu. Kungani kuyokuba nomvuzo ekugcineni?`
