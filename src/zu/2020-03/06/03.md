---
title:  'UNkulunkulu: Ophana Ngazo Zonke IziphoEzinhle'
date:  03/08/2020
---

Ngokubhalwe ku 1 Korinte 12:11, 18; Efesu 4:7, 8; noJakobe 1:17, uNkulunkulu unguMqambi wezipho zonke, futhi “sonke isipho esiphelele sivela kuYena.” Kanjaloke singaqiniseka ukuthi uzosipha lezo zipho zikaMoya oNgcwele ezifanele izimilo zethu, asebenzise amakhono ethu ngendlela okuyiyona efanelekile umsebenzini waKhe, kudunyiswe igama laKhe.

`Funda kuMarku 13:34 no 1 Korinte 12:;11.  UNkulunkulu uzinika bani izipho zikaMoya na?`

IBhayibheli licacile.  UNkulunkulu unomsebenzi okhethekile wokuthi omunye nomunye kithina sabele abanye ngevangeli. Endabeni kaJesu yomninimuzi owashiya indlu yakhe nezisebenzi, wazitshela ukuba zinakekele yonke into, umninimuzi wanika izisebenzi zakhe imisebenzi (Marko 13:34).  Kukhona umsebenzi womuntu ngamunye, kanti uNkulunkulu unika izipho zikaMoya kubo bonke ukuze bafeze umsebenzi abawubizelwe.  Uma sinikela ngezimpilo zethu kuKrestu, sibe amalunga omzimba waKhe, ibandla, ngokubhabhadiswa, uMoya oNgcwele uphana ngezipho ukuze sikwazi ukukhonza umzimba, sifakaze emhlabeni. Ngo 1903, uEllen G. White wabhalela indoda ethile incwadi, eyikhuthaza ukuthi isebenzise izipho eziphiwe nguNkulunkulu ukumkhonza. “Sonke singamalunga omndeni kaNkulunkulu, sonke siphathiswe amathalente amakhulu noma amancane esiyophendula ngendlela esiwasebenzise ngayo.  Noma ithalente lakho likhulu noma lincane, kudingeka ulisebenzisele ukukhonza uNkulunkulu, futhi asilamukele ilungelo lomunye umuntu lokusebenzisa izipho aziphathisiwe. “Masingalokothi sibukele phansi isipho somzimba, somqondo, nesomphefumulo noma sisincane kangakanani.”—Letter 260, December 2, 1903.

`Funda kuZenzo 10:36-38, Mathewu 3:16-18, neZenzo 2:38-42. Lezindimana zisifundisani ngesithembiso sikaMoya oNgcwele ekubhabhadisweni?`

Ngendlela efanayo uJesu agcotshwa ngayo nguMoya oNgcwele ekubhabhadisweni kwaKhe ukumlungisa nokumhlomisa ngokuphelele ukwenza umsebenzi waKhe emhlabeni, sonke simthenjisiwe uMoya oNgcwele ngesikhathi sokubhabhadiswa kwethu. UNkulunkulu ulangazelela ukuthi sibe nesiqiniseko sokuthi uligcwalisile iZwi laKhe, wasipha izipho zikaMoya ukuze sibusise ibandla laKhe nomhlaba.
