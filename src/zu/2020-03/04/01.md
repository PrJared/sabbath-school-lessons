### Kuleliviki Funda ku
ISamb. 12:7-9, Efesu 6:12, Heb. 7:25, Efesu 1:15-21, Dan. 10:10-14, 1 Johane 6:14-16.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Ngakho, vumani izono omunye komunye, nikhulekelane ukuba niphulukiswe. Umkhuleko oqinileyo wolungileyo unamandla kakhulu” (Jakobe 5:16).

Ibandla lesikhathi seTestamente eliSha lasizwa isidingo salo somthandazo. “Kwathi sebekhulekile, kwazanyazanyiswa indawo lapho bebebuthene khona, bagcwaliswa bonke nguMoya oNgcwele, bakhuluma iZwi likaNkulunkulu ngesibindi” (IZenzo 4:31). Qaphela, abafundi bathandaza.  Babegcwaliswe nguMoya oNgcwele, base bekhuluma iZwi likaNkulunkulu ngesibindi noma ngokuzethemba.

Kwakukhona ubudlelwane ubuthe ngqo phakathi kwemithandazo yabo, ukugcwaliswa nguMoya oNgcwele, nokumemezela iZwi likaNkulunkulu ngamandla.  “Abafundi …abazicelelanga isibusiso. Babenomthwalo ngokusindiswa kwemiphefumulo. Baqonda ukuthi ivangeli kumelwe liyiswe emhlabeni, bacela lawomandla uKrestu ayewathembisile.”—Ellen G. White, The Acts of the Apostles, ikhasi 37.

Uma sifuna uNkulunkulu, sinxusela nabanye, uNkulunkulu uyasebenza ezinhliziyweni zethu, asidonsele eduze naYe, asiphe ukuhlakanipha kwasezulwini, sibazuzele umbuso waKhe. (Jakobe 1:5).  Uphinde asebenze ngamandla ezimpilweni zabo ngendlela esingeke siyibone noma siyiqonde ngokuphelele, ukubadonsela kuYe (1 Johane 5:14-17).

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungisela iSabatha lika Ntulikazi 25._
