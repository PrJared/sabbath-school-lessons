### Kuleliviki Funda ku
Johane 15:26, 27; IZenzo 2:41, IZenzo 18:8.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Sebekhulekile, kwazanyazanyiswa indawo lapho bebebuthene khona, bagcwaliswa bonke nguMoya oNgcwele, bakhuluma izwi likaNkulunkulu ngesibindi” (IZenzo 4:31).

Ngesikhathi uJesu eyala abazalwane  bokuqala ukuba “baye kuwo wonke umhlaba, bashumayele ivangeli,” kufanele ukuthi kwabonakala njengento engenakwenzeka (Marko 16:15).  Babengayifeza kanjani-nje lenselele engako? Babembalwa.  Izinsiza zokusebenza zazingengakanani. Imvamisa yabo babengabantu abangafundile.  Kodwa babenoNkulunkulu ongavamile, owayezobapha amandla okwenza umsebenzi ongavamile.

Kodwa uJesu wathi: “Kodwa nizakwamukeliswa amandla, uMoya oNgcwele esefikile phezu kwenu, nibe ngofakazi baMi eJerusalema, naseJudiya lonke, naseSamariya kuze kube sekugcineni komhlaba” (IZenzo 1:8).  Ukunikeza kwamandla nguMoya oNgcwele kwakuzobenza babelane ngesigijimi sesiphambano ngamandla aguqula impilo, aguqula umhlaba. UMoya oNgcwele wenza ukuthi ubufakazi babo busebenze. Ngeminyaka embalwa-nje, ivangeli laselifinyelele emhlabeni wonke. EyeZenzo ithi labazalwane bokuqala “bagumbuqela umhlaba” (IZenzo 17:6, NKJV).  Umphostoli uPawulu wenezela ngokuthi ivangeli lase “lishunyayelwe kuzo zonke izidalwa ngaphansi kwezulu” (Kolose 1:23, NKJV)). Kulesifundo saleliviki sizogxila ngokukhethekile kwindima kaMoya oNgcwele yokusinika amandla ekufakazeleni uKrestu.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungisela iSabatha lika Ncwaba 1._
