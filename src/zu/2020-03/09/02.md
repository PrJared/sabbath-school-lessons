---
title:  Ukwamukeleka Kwevangeli
date:  23/08/2020
---

`Funda kuJohane 4:27-30, 39-42. Ukuhlangana kukaJesu nowesifazane waseSamariya kulitshengisa kanjani iqiniso lokuthi zonke izinhlobo zabantu zivulekele ivangeli, nasezindaweni ezingalindelekile?`

Indawo yokugcina abafundi ababengalindela kuyona ukuthola izinhliziyo ezivulelekele ivangeli, kwakuseSamariya. AmaSamariya ayehlala ephikisana namaJuda ngezimfundiso nangokukhonza. Lokhu kungezwani kwase kuneminyaka eminingi. AmaSamariya ayekade efuna ukwelekelela ekwakhiweni kwethempeli eJerusalema, kodwa enqatshelwa lelo thuba ngenxa yokuganiselana kwawo nezizwe zabahedeni kanye nendlela ababechaza ngayo izwi. Umphumela walokho kwaba ukuthi amaSamariya azakhela ithempeli lawo entabeni iGerizimu. Abafundi babekubona kulula ukuyeqa iSamariya njengendawo engavundile ekushunyayelweni kwevangeli. UJesu wabona lokho abafundi ababengakuboni: izinhliziyo ezivumayo. UJohane uqala ngalamazwi uma ebhala ngalendaba: “Wasuka eJudiya, waphindela eGalile.  Kodwa kwakudingeke ukuthi adabule eSamariya” (Johane 4:3, 4, NKJV). UJesu “kwakudingekile” ukuthi adabule eSamariya ngoba uMoya oNgcwele wamtshela ukuthi kukhona izinhliziyo ezilungele ukwamukela ivangeli kulendawo engalindelekile. Uma amehlo ethu egcotshwe nguMoya oNgcwele, sibona amathuba lapho khona abanye bebona ubunzima kuphela.  Sibona isivuno esikhulu semiphefumulo sombuso kaNkulunkulu lapho abanye bebona kuphela ugwadule.

`Funda kuZenzo 8:4, 5, 14. Ekugcineni waba yini umphumela womsebenzi kaJesu eSamariya na?`

Abafundi babezodlula eSamariya ngaphandle kokunika abaseSamariya ithuba lokuzwa iqiniso leZwi likaNkulunkulu.  UJesu wabona lokho ababengakuboni. Wabona ukuthi uMoya oNgcwele wayedale isimo sokwamukela ivangeli enhliziyweni yalo wesifazane. Ukuphenduka kwakhe kwaba nomthelela ebantwini abaningi kulelo dolobha.  Ngeke sibone imiphumela esheshayo sonke isikhathi ngokufakaza kwethu, kodwa uma silokhu sitshala imbewu ezinhliziyweni ezilungele ukuyamukela, ngelinye ilanga iyothela isivuno sodumo lukaNkulunkulu.

`Asiwazi ngokuqinisekile umthelela omuhle noma omubi, wamazwi ethu nezenzo zethu kwabanye abantu.  Ngakhoke, kungani kufanele njalo-nje siqaphele esikushoyo nesikwenzayo phambi kwabanye abantu?`
