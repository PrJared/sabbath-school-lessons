---
title:  Popeho ea Daniele
date:  30/12/2019
---

Tlhopheho ea karolo ea se-Aramaic ea Daniele, khaolo ea 2–7 (likarolo tse ling tsa Daniele li ne li le ka se-Heberu ha tse ling li le ka se-Aramaic), e bontša popeho e latelang, e thusang ho tiisa molaetsa oa mantlha oa karolo eo, le oa buka:

- A. pono ea Nebukadnesare ea mebuso e mene (Daniele 2)
    - B. Molimo o lopolla balekane ba Daniele seboping sa mollo (Daniele 3)
    - C. Kahlolo holim’a Nebukadnesare (Daniele 4)
    - C’. Kahlolo holim’a Belshatsare (Daniele 5)
    - B’. Molimo o lopolla Daniele lemeneng la litau (Daniele 6)
- A’. Pono ea Daniele ea mebuso e mene (Daniele 7)

Mofuta ona oa tlhophiso ea bongoli o hloaea ntlha ea bohlokoa ka ho e beha khubung ea tlhophiso, boemong bona e leng ho C le C’(Daniele 4 le 5): Molimo o tlosa ‘muso ho Nebukadnesare (ha nakoana) le ho Belshatsare (ka botlalo). Kahona, thatiselletso ea khoalo ea 2–7 e holim’a borena ba Molimo holim’a marena a lefatše haa a hlomamisa a bile a a tlosa. E ‘ngoe ea litsela tse sebetsang hantle haholo ea ho hlakisa ntlha ke pheta-pheto. Mohlala, Molimo o fa Faro toro tse peli ka bokamoso bo haufi ba Egepeta (Gen. 41:1–7). Torong ea pele, likhomo tse supileng tse nonneng li metsoa ke likhomo tse supileng tse otileng. Torong ea bobeli liqoapi tse nonneng tsa koro li metsoa ke liqoapi tse otileng. Litoro tsena ka bobeli li bua taba e le ‘ngoe: lilemo tse supileng tsa khora li tla lateloa ke lilemo tse supileng tsa tlala. Bukeng ea Daniele, le teng Molimo o sebelisa pheta-pheto. Ho na le lilikalikoe tse ‘ne tsa boprofeta, tseo e leng pheta-pheto ea tlhophiso ea mantlha e akaretsang. Qetellong, tlhophiso ena e re bontša borena ba Molimo bo kaholimo-limo. Leha tlaleho ka ‘ngoe ea boprofeta e hlahisa lehlakore le ikhethollang, hammoho li akaretsa nako e le ‘ngoe ea nalane, e tlohang nakong ea moprofeta ho isa bofelong, joalo kaha tafole e latelang e bontša:
