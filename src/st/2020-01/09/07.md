Joalo kaha re bona mona, ho na le ho bapa ha litaba likhaolong tsena. Lichaba ha lia hlahisoa feela ka tsela e bapileng, empa ketsahalo ea kahlolo e ho Daniele 7—e hlahang kamorao ho lilemo tse 1,260 (A.D.538–1798 B.C.) tsa Roma ea bopapa, e bapa le nako ea tlhoekiso ea sehalalelo, eo ho Daniele 8 e hlahang kamor’a Roma, le eona. Ka bokhutšoanyane, kahlolo ena e leholimong ho Daniele 7—kahlolo e lebisang bofelong ba lefatše—ke ntho e le ‘ngoe le ho hloekisoa ha sehalalelo ho Daniele 8. Mona re fuoe litšoantšo tse peli tsa ntho e tšoanang, ‘me ka bobeli li etsahala kamorao ho nako ea lilemo tse 1,260 tsa litlhoriso tse tlisoang ke ‘muso oa lenaka le lenyane.

**Lipotso tsa Puisano**

`1.	Tafole e kaholimo e re bontša joang hore ho hloekisoa ha sehalalelo, ntho e le ‘ngoe le kahlolo ho Daniele 7, li lokela ho etshala nako kamor’a boprofeta ba lilemo tse 1,260 ba lenaka le lenyane, feela pele ho hlongoa ha ‘muso oa ho qetela oa Molimo?`

`2.	Boprofeta ba Daniele 8 bo bontša nalane e le ntho e ‘ngoe e likhoka hape e tletseng bokhopo. Liphoofolo tse peli tse tšoantšang mebuso ea lefatše lia loantšana (Dan. 8:8–12). ‘Muso oa lenaka le lenyenyaneo hlahang kamor’a tsona, ke ‘muso o likhoka o bolaeang (Dan. 8:23–25).ka mokhoa o joalo, Lengolo mona le etsa teko ea ho theolela kutloisisong ea rona bonnete ba mahlomola a lafatšeng lena. Taba ee e ka re thusa ho tšepa Molimo joang le molemo oa Hae le har’a bokhopo boo re bo bonang bo re pota-potile?`
