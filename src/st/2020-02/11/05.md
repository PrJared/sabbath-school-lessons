---
title:  Kahlolo ea Patlisiso
date:  10/06/2020
---

Tlhaloso ea boithuto ba boporofeta bo ithutoang bekeng ena bo fumane tšehetso e hlollang hara maporosetanta a tlhaloso ka nalane ha e sa le ho tloha ka nchafatso. Empa ha e-ea ka e aba ho fihlela ka sehlopha sa Millerite maqalong a bo-1800s moo matsatsi a 2,300 le kahlolo ea patlisiso li ileng tsa shejoa le jo ithutoa ka hloko. Sheba lenane le latelang:

|Daniele 7 | Daniele 8|
|---|---|
|Babylona (tau) | -|
|Media-Persia (bere) | Media-Persia (pheleu)|
|Greece (lengau) | Greece (phooko)|
|Roma ea bohetene (sebata sa bone) | Roma ea bohetene (manaka a a tšekaletse)|
|Roma ea Bopapa (lenakana le lenyenyane) | Roma ea bopapa (manaka a ea nyoloha)|

`Bala Daniele 7:9–14; 8:14, 26. Ho etsahala eng leholimong joalo ka ho bontšitsoe litemaneng tsee?`

Ka mora nako ea litlhoriso tsa nako ea bohareng, tse qeteletseng ka 1798 ka ho tšoaroa le ho koalloa ha mopapa ke General Berthier (Tšen. 13:3), Daniele 7 le 8 e bua ka kahlolo. Kahlolo e lokela ho etsahala leholimong moo ‘“lekhotla le lutseng’“ (Dan. 7:10) ’me ‘“E mong ea kang Mora oa motho, a tla ka maru a leholimo . . . a tla ho Moholo oa Matsatsi’“ (Dan. 7:13). Ena ke pono ea kahlolo e etsahalang ka mora 1798 le pele ho ho khutla labobeli ha Jesu. Pono ena ea kahlolo ho Daniele 7 e lekana hantle le tloekiso ea sehalalelo ho Daniele 8:14. Li bua ka ntho e le ’ngoe. Ho ea ka Daniele 8:14, nako ena ea “tlhoekiso ea sehalalelo,” e leng letsatsi la pheko, ke 2,300 e baloang mantsiboea le hosasa. Ka motheo oa selemo-letsatsi, matsatsi ana a emela lilemo tse 2,300. Ntlha ea ho qala ea lilemo tse 2,300 e fumanoa ho Daniele 9:24, moo libeke tse 70 (lilemo tse 490) tsa boporofeta e eng chatak, kapa “khaotsoe,” ho tsoa ponong ea matsatsi a 2,300-day (Dan. 9:24). Hantle, baithuti ba bangata hantle ba bona boporofeta ba matsatsi(lilemo) a 2,300 a Daniele 8:14 le boporofeta ba libeke tse 70 (lilemo 490) a Daniele 9:24–27 e le likarolo tse peli tsa ntho e le ’ngoe. Temana e latelang boporofeteng ba libeke tse 70, Daniele 9:25, e fana ka qaleho ea nako, ‘“ho tloha taelong e reng Jerusalema o tla boela tsosoe a hahuoe’“. Nako ea ketsahalo ena ke ka selemo sa bosupa morena rtaxarxe” (Ezra 7:7), kapa 457 B.C. ho bala ho ea pele lilemo tse 2,300, re fihlile ho 1844, e seng khale ka mora 1798 le pele ho ho khutla la bobeli ha Jesu. Sena e ne e le ha Jesu a kena ka sehalalelisiso le ho qala mosebetsi oa hae o abo boelelli, oa ho hloekisa sehalalelo sa leholimo. Sheba lenane thuto la thuto ea Labohlano.
