---
title:  Ibandla Elinikwe Amandla
date:  27/07/2020
---

Incwadi yeIZenzo ibizwe ngokufaneleyo, “IZenzo zikaMoya Oyingcwele.” Liphulo elivuyisayo ekungqineni, ekuvakaliseni iindaba ezilungileyo, nokukhula kwebandla. IZenzo libali lamakholwa azinikeleyo, azaliswe nguMoya Oyingcwele, anempembelelo emhlabeni ngenxa kaKristu. Ayexhomekeke ngokupheleleyo kuMoya Oyingcwele ukuba abe neziphumo ezingummangaliso. Okwawo ngumzekelo wento enokwenziwa ngokusebenza kwamadoda nabafazi abazinikele ngokupheleleyo kuYe.

`Funda: IZenzo 2:41, 42; IZenzo 4:4, 31; IZenzo 5:14, 42; IZenzo 6:7; neIZenzo 16:5. Yintoni etsala umdla wakho kakhulu ngezi ndawo? Sithini isigidimi uLuka, umbhali weIZenzo, anga angabelana ngaso ngokubhala ngokukhula okukhawuleze kangako?`

Injongo kaLuka ekubhaleni incwadi yeIZenzo kukwabelana nomfundi ngamnye ngolungiselelo lukaMoya Oyingcwele ebandleni lokuqala.  Qaphela, kwakhona, ukuba akaphazami ukusebenzisa amanani ukulinganisa ukusebenza kukaMoya Oyingcwele ngenkulungwane yokuqala. Kungoko wayebala ababebhaptizwa. Kwi-IZenzo 2:41, ubabaza ukuba ama-3,000 abhaptizwa ngemini enye kwindawo enye. Kwi-IZenzo 4:4, uthetha ngama-5,000 abantu ababhaptizwayo. Kwi-IZenzo 5:14, izihlwele ziza eNkosini zibhaptizwe.

Nokuba ngumntu omnye njengoLidiya, umgcini-mabanjwa eFilipi, intwazana elikhoboka enedemon, okanye ithenwa laseTiyopiya, uLuka uyakuqaphela akubhale ukusebenza kukaMoya Oyingcwele ezintliziyweni zaba bantu. Indawo ebalulekileyo apha yile yokuba, ngaphaya kwenani ngalinye elikhulu labantu, elowo nalowo ungumntwana kaThixo owafelwa nguYesu Kristu. Kunjalo, siyawathanda amanani amakhulu, kodwa ekugqibeleni, ukungqina kusoloko kungumzamo womntu ojongene nomnye. Ukukhuthaza ukukhula okukhawulezileyo kwebandla leTestamene eNtsha, kwatyalwa amabandla amatsha. Esinye sezizathu sokukhula kwebandla lokuqala ngokukhawuleza okukhulu kungenxa yokuba ibandla lalithe rhoqo lihlaziywa ngokutyalwa kwamabandla amatsha. Isigidimi esibaluleke kangako kuthi namhlanje!

Ingqwalaselo ephambili yebandla leTestamente eNtsha yayikuvangelo.

`Singaqinisekisa njani ukuba, embindini wako konke esikwenzayo ebandleni lokuhlala, uvangelo lusoloko lusembindini [lungumongo]?`
