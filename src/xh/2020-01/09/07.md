Njengoko sibona apha, kukho ukuhambelana phakathi kwezahluko. Asikuphela nje kweentlanga eziboniswe zihambelana, nomboniso womgwebo kuDaniyeli 7-obekho emva kweminyaka eli-1,260 (A.D.538–1798 B.C.) yeRoma yobupopi, nayo ihambelana nokuhlanjululwa kwengcwele, ethi, kuDaniyeli 8 ivuke nayo emva kweRoma. Ngokufutshane, lo mgwebo wasezulwini ukhokelela ekupheleni komhlaba-yinto enye nokuhlanjululwa kwengcwele okukuDaniyeli 8. Sinikwe apha imiboniso emibini eyahlukeneyo yento enye, yaye yomibini yenzeka emva kwexesha leminyaka elili-1,260 lentshutshiso eyenziwe mbi ligunya lophondo oluncinane.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxwa**

`1. Le tshathi ingentla isibonisa njani ukuba ukuhlanjululwa kwengcwele, into enye nomgwebo okuDaniyeli 7, kufuneka kwenzeke emva kwesiprofeto se-1,260 leminyaka yophondo oluncinane, kodwa ngaphambi kokumiselwa kobukumkani bokugqibela bukaThixo?`

`2. Isiprofeto sikaDaniyeli 8 sibonisa imbali njengento enobubhovubhovu nezele bububi. Izilwanyana ezibini, ezifuzisela izikumkani zomhlaba ezibini, ziyalwa esinye nesinye (Dan. 8:8–12). Igunya eliliphondo elincinane eliphuma emva kwazo liligunya elinobungonyama nelitshutshisayo (Dan. 8:23–25). Ngoko ke, isiBhalo apha senza umzamo wokubuthethela phantsi ubunyaniso bokubulaleka kweli hlabathi. Kungasinceda njani ukufunda ukuthembela kuThixo nokulunga kwaKhe nangona ubunyaniso bobubi sibubona busijikelezile?`
