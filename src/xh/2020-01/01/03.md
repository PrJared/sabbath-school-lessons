---
title:  Ukwakhiwa Kwencwadi KaDaniyeli
date:  30/12/2019
---

Ukuhlelwa kwendawo kaDaniyeli ebhalwe ngolwimi iAramaic, izahluko 2-7 (ezinye iindawo zikaDaniyeli zibhalwe ngesiHebhere lo gama ezinye zibhalwe ngesiAramaic), kuveza oku kumila kulandelayo, nokuthi kusincede ukubethelela isigidimi esingumongo waloo ndawo, kunye nowencwadi:

- A. Umbono kaNebhukadenetsare wezikumkani ezine (Daniyeli 2)
    - B. UThixo uhlangula amaqabane kaDaniyeli ezikweni Lomlilo (Daniyeli 3)
        - C. Ukugwetywa kukaNebhukadenetsare (Daniyeli 4)
        - C’. Ukugwetywa kukaBheleshatsare (Daniyeli 5)
    - B’. UThixo uhlangula uDaniyeli emhadini weengonyama (Daniyeli 6)
- A’. Umbono kaDaniyeli wezikumkani ezine (Daniyeli 7)

Olu hlobo lokuhlela ekubhaleni lunceda ukugqamisa ingongoma eyintloko ngokuyibeka embindini wesakhiwo, apha ke ongu-C no-C’ (Daniyeli 4 no-5): UThixo ususa ubukumkani kuNebhukadenetsare (okwexeshana) nakuBheleshatsare (umphelo). Ngoko ke, ugxininiso lwezahluko 2-7 lusegunyeni likaThixo phezu kookumkani bomhlaba njengoko ebamisela ekwabasusa. Enye yeendlela ezikwazi kakuhle ukuzisa isigidimi nokuyenza icaceingongoma kukuphindaphinda. Umzekelo, uThixo unika uFaro amaphupha amabini ngekusasa elingekude leYiputa (Gen. 41:1–7). Kwiphupha lokuqala, iimazi ezityebileyo ezisixhenxe zidliwa zezibhityileyo ezisixhenxe. Kwiphupha lesibini, izikhwebu ezisixhenxe zengqolowa entle ziginywa sisixhenxe sezingcakacileyo nezitshazileyo. Omabini la maphupha athetha into enye: iminyaka esixhenxe yentlutha iya kulandelwa yesixhenxe yendlala. Kwincwadi kaDaniyeli, uThixo ukwasebenzisa ukuphinda- phinda. Mine imijikelezo yesiprofeto, ekukuphinda-phindwa kwako konke okuthethiweyo. Ekugqibeleni, konke oku kubonisa isiphelo esikukongama kukaThixo. Nangona umjikelezo ngamnye omkhulu wesiprofeto usiza nomba othe qabavu wodwa, xa ihlangene ilithabatha lonke ixesha lembali, ukusuka kwixesha lomprofeti kuse ekupheleni, njengoko lo mzobo ulandelayo ubonisa:
