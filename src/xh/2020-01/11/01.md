### Fundela Isifundo Sale Veki
Efese 6:12, Daniyeli 10, Ezra 4:1–5, Yosh. 5:13–15, IsiTy. 1:12–18, Kol. 2:15, Roma 8:37–39.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Wena ndoda ethandwe kakhulu, musa ukoyika! Yiba noxolo; yomelela, ewe, yomelela! (Daniyeli 10:19, NKJV).

UDaniyeli 10 ungenisa umboniso wokuphela kukaDaniyeli, esiqhubeka kwisahluko se-11 nese-12. Sixelelwa zisuka ukuba lo mbono ungo“zengezenge ke olukhulu” (Dan. 10:1). Lo gama uDaniyeli 11 efaka inyama yeenkcukacha yeli dabi, uDaniyeli 10 ubonisa amacala asemoyeni aveze ukuba, ngaphaya kwemiboniso yamadabi asemhlabeni kukho idabi lasemoyeni elilelezwe lonke. Xa sifunda esi sahluko siza kubona ukuba xa sithandaza, siquka olu ngquzulwano lwezwe lonke ngendlela oluneziphumo ezididayo. Phofu asisedwa ekulweni kwethu; uYesu usedabini noSathana ngenxa yethu. Siza kufunda ukuba umlo esikuwo wokugqibela awulwi namagunya abantu basemhlabeni kodwa namagunya obumnyama.

Ngokutsho kompostile uPawulos sekuziinkulungwane emva kukaDaniyeli: “Kuba kweli dabi lethu asilwi nabantu, kodwa silwa nemimoya ekhohlakeleyo esesibhakabhakeni, izilawuli, nabasemagunyeni, kunye nemimoya engcolileyo yeli phakade lobumnyama” (Efese 6:12, IBHAYIBHILE). Ekugqibeleni, impumelelo yethu kweli dabi ihleli phezu kukaYesu Kristu, onguYe yedwa owoyise uSathana emnqamlezweni.
