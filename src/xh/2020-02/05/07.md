Funda isahluko 20, “Bible Teaching and Study,” in the book Education, pp. 185–192; “The Primacy of the Word” in Selected Messages, book 3, pp. 29–33. “Umfundi weBhayibhile kufuneka afundiswe ukuza kuyo ngomoya womfundi. Kufuneka siphengulule amaphepha ayo, hayi ukufumana ubungqina bokuxhasa iimbono zethu, kodwa ukuze sikwazi oko akuthethayo uThixo. Ulwazi lokwenene ngeBhayibhile lunokuzuzwa kuphela ngoncedo lwaloMoya eyanikezelwa ngawo. Kwaye ukuze siluzuze olu lwazi kufuneka siphile ngalo. Konke oko ilizwi likaThixo likuyalelayo, kufuneka sikuthobele. . . . Ukufundwa kweBhayibhile kudinga owona mzamo wethu unyamekileyo nengcinga ezingisayo. Njengomombi-mgodi esemba ubutyebi obuxabisekileyo emhlabeni, kufuneka nathi sibufune ngenyameko, nangokuzingileyo ubutyebi belizwi likaThixo.”—Ellen G. White, Education, p. 189.  “Xa iBhayibhile uyenza ukudla kwakho, inyama yakho, nesiselo sakho, xa imiyalelo uyenza iziqalelo zesimilo sakho, uyakwazi ngcono indlela yokufumana icebiso kuThixo. Ndiphakamisa ilizwi elixabisekileyo phambi kwakho namhlanje. Sukuphinda oko ndikuthethileyo, usithi, ‘Udade White uthetha oku, waza ‘udade White wathetha okuya.’ Zifumanele ngokwakho oko iNkosi uThixo kaSirayeli ikuthethayo, uze wenza oko ikuyalelayo.”—Ellen G. White, Selected Messages, book 3, p. 33.

**Imibuzo Yengxoxo**

`1. Zeziphi iinkolo eziyimposiso ababambelele kuzo abantu kuba bekhangele kuphela kwindima ezimbalwa ezityunjiweyo endaweni yako konke oko iBhayibhile ikuthethayo ngalo mxholo?`

`2. KuMateyu 11:11, uYesu wathi ngoYohane umBhaptizi: “Inene ndithi kuni, Phakathi kwabo bazelweyo ngabafazi, akuvelanga mkhulu kunoYohane umbhaptizi; kanti ke nomncinane kwabanye ebukumkanini bamazulu mkhulu kunaye. UYesu apha ukhomba kumprofethi ongenamibhalo eBhayibhileni kodwa wathetha izinto ezingako ngaye. Oku makusixelele ntoni ngokuba kutheni umprofeti wokwenene kungadingeki ukuba abe nencwadi eBhayibhileni kwaye kunjalo abe ngumprofeti wenene? Myalezo mni esinokuwufumana apha njengaBalindi kule nyaniso?`

`3. NjengaBalindi, asisedwa ekubangeni iBhayibhile njengegunya lethu lokugqibela. Namanye amabandla enza ngolohlobo nawo. Sizicacisa njani, ngoko, iimfundiso ezikhabanayo amanye amabandla abanga ukuba azifumene eBhayibheleni, nawo?`
