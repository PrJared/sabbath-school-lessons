---
title:  Ufuziselo njengeSiprofeto
date:  11/06/2020
---

Imifuziselo yesiprofeto sokuphela kwexesha, efana naleyo ifumaneka kuDaniyeli neSiTyhilelo, inokuzaliseka okunye. Umzekelo, inkunzi yebhokwe izaliseke kumaGrike, ubukumkani obubodwa (Dan. 8:21). Kakade, indima yaphuhla yasibizela ngegama! Yeyiphi enye indlela ebinokucaca ngayo?  Ufuziselo, noko kunjalo, igxila kubantu gaqa, iziganeko, okanye amaziko eTestamente eNdala esekelwe kwinyaniso yembali kodwa ekhangele phambili kwinyaniso yokwenene kwixa elizayo. Ukusetyenzisa kofuziselo njengendlela yokupicotha ibuyela emva kuYesu nababhali beTestamente eNtsha, yaye iyafumaneka nkqu nakwiTestamente eNdala. Esona sikhokelo ekuphela kwaso sokuqonda umfuziselo kwisiqu kuxa umbhali ophefumlelweyo wesiBhalo ezikhankanya.

`Funda 1 Korinte 10:1–13. Usingisele kweziphi iziganeko zembali xa ekhuthaza ibandla laseKorinte? Oku kudibana njani nathi namhlanje?`

UPawulos walatha emva kwinyaniso eyimbali ye-Eksodus aze asungule ufuziselo olusekelwe kumava amaHebhere amandulo entlango. Ngale ndlela, uPawulos ubonisa ukuba uThixo, owaphefumlela uMoses ukuba ashicilele ezi ziganeko, wamisela ukuba “zibe yimizekelo kuthi” (1 Kor 10:16), ngokukhuthaza uSirayeli wasemoyeni ukuba anyamezele isihendo njengoko siphila kwimihla yokugqibela.

**Funda ezi ndima zilandelayo uze ubhale phantsi ukuzaliseka komfuziselo nesiqu ngasinye, njengoko kuchaziwe nguYesu nababhali beTestamente eNtsha.**

`Mat. 12:40` 

`Yohane 19:36`

`Yohane 3:14, 15`

`Rom. 5:14` 

`Yohane 1:29`

Kwimeko nganye, uYesu nababhali beTestamente eNtsha basebenzisa ukupicotha komfuziselo nesiqu ezivumela ukubaluleka ngokwesiprofeto kuvele poqo. Ngale ndlela, zalatha kwinzaliseko yenyaniso yembali.
