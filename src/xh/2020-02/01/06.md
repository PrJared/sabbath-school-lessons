---
title:  Amandla Aguqulayo eLizwi
date:  02/04/2020
---

`Funda 2 Kumkani 22:3–20. Yintoni ebangela ukuba uKumkani uYosiya akrazule iingubo zakhe? Oko akubhaqileyo kuguqula njani hayi nje yena kuphela, kodwa lonke uhlanga lakwaYuda?`

Ngo-621 B.C., xa uYosiya wayekuma-25 ubudala, uHelekiya, umbingeleli omkhulu, wabhaqa “incwadi yomyalelo,” ekunokwenzeka ukuba yayizincwadi ezintlanu zokuqala zikaMoses okanye, ngokutsolileyo, incwadi yeDuteronomi. Ngexa lokulawula kukayise u-Amon, noyisemkhulu wakhe okhohlakele ngeyona ndlela uManasse, le ncwadi yalahleka esizikithini sokunqulwa kukaBhahali, Ashera, nomkhosi wonke wezulu” (2 Kumkani 21:3–9). Uthe xa uYosiya esiva imiqathango yomnqophiso, wakrazula iingubo zakhe ngenxa yokusindeka okumandla, kuba eqonda indlela yena nabantu bakhe ebemke ngayo ekunquleni uThixo wokwenene. Ngoko nangoko waqalisa uhlaziyo elizweni lonke jikelele, ediliza iziganga ekwatshabalalisa nemifanekiso yoothixo basemzini. Esakugqiba, inye kuphela indawo eseleyo yokunqula kwaYuda: yitempile kaThixo eYerusalem. Ukubhaqa iLizwi likaThixo kukhokelela kukweyiseka, inguquko kunye namandla otshintsho. Le nguqu iqala ngoYosiya ize kamva inwenwele kuYuda xa ewonke.

`IBhayibhile isiqinisekisa njani ukuba inamandla okuguqula ubomi bethu ize isibonise indlela eya elusindisweni? Funda uYohane 16:13, Yohane 17:17, Hebhere 4:12, namaRoma 12:2.`

Esinye sezingqino ezinamandla obunye bobungqina obuninzi obunamandla bamandla eBhayibhile bubomi obutshintshileyo bomntu. LiLizwi iligqobhoza kwisono nokungcola koluntu lize lityhile imvelo yethu yobuntu yokwenene nokudinga kwethu uMsindisi. Incwadi eyahluke njengeBhayibhile, equlathwe kwimbali, yazaliswa ngesiprofetho, namandla okuguqula ubomi, kufuneka kanjalo ipicothwe ngendlela eyahlukileyo. Ayikwazi kupicothwa njengayo nayiphi incwadi, kuba iLizwi eliphilileyo likaThixo kufuneka liqondwe kukhangelwe kuKristu ophilileyo othembisa ngokuthumela uMoya waKhe ukuba asikhokelele “kuyo yonke inyaniso” (Yohane 16:13). IBhayibhile, ngoko, njengentyilelo yenyaniso kaThixo, kufuneka iqulathe imimiselo yayo yokupicotha engaphakathi kuyo. Le mimiselo inokufumaneka ngokufunda indlela ababhali besiBhalo abasisebenzisa ngayo isiBhalo baza bakhokelwa siso xa babevumela isiBhalo ukuba sizipicotho ngokunokwaso.
